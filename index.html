About World Information Architecture Day

World IA Day is an accessible one-day conference aimed at bringing the Information Architecture community together.

Come February 20th, 2016, we’ll be celebrating our fifth annual World IA Day in many cities across the globe. It's a fun day to learn from world-class IA minds, network with people passionate about IA, and showcase your new IA-related ideas. It's also a fantastic opportunity to have talks and content tailored specifically to your local community and in your local language.

Sound good? We hope your city host World IA Day in your city. If you have strong leadership skills and are excited to tailor World IA Day for your community, big or small, submit your application by June 20, 2015.

The basics of running your local World IA Day

We believe local leadership and autonomy in decision-making is key to successful, culturally relevant events.

We recommend you start thinking about the operations of your local event shortly after you apply. Each year, organizers run it a bit differently, but generally we see successful event timelines run something like this…

  1. Build a local team: You start building a team by connecting with a few folks in your community who are interested in filling out the call for cities proposal with you. The Information Architecture Institute reviews all submissions and notifies the cities of whether or not they are accepted. Once accepted, you can grow your team and plan your event with the support of the Information Architecture Institute global leaders.
  2. You check in with global. The Information Architecture Institute global team will check in with you regularly to make sure you’re getting the support you need. We will let you know what other local leaders are doing across the globe and help you problem-solve any issues that arise. We’ll provide guidelines on when and how things could be done to help you if you’re new to event planning.
  3. You manage your own event logistics. While we don’t mandate event management in a certain way, we know a general planning schedule might look something like this:
    July
    Recruit your volunteer team
    August
    Identify sponsors and speakers from your community
    September
    Determine venue & audio/visual needs
    October
    Publish final details on the World IA Day website
    November
    Market the event to your community
    December
    Design the day-of experience for your event
    January
    Open registration and finalize day-of details
  4. You host your event! On February 20th, 2016, you’ll host your very own World IA Day - the format and content is up to you. The day can be filled with talks, roundtable chats, workshops, or something we haven’t even thought of yet. The only thing that we ask is that you keep your event accessible to all in your community and ours. The Information Architecture Institute global team will help promote your tweets, live streams, and video uploads as your event takes place.

At each step, we will provide advice, templates, toolkits, and any other planning tools you request to make sure that you're well equipped to run your event.

Conditions for running your own event

The Information Architecture Institute global team has a few conditions we hope applying cities will abide by to make sure that all World IA Day events are connected and successful. The following are a set of guidelines that we hope you will abide by:

  1. Accessible for any attendee with a desire to participate
  2. Commitment to pursuing local sponsors to cover event costs
  3. Promotion of World IA Day as an Information Architecture Institute event
  4. Promotion of global sponsors before and during the event
  5. The rights for Information Architecture Institute to publish your event content
  6. Commitment to providing the following post-event:
    • Post-event survey
    • Attendee numbers & data
    • Itemized budget
    • Any overage dollars donated to Information Architecture Institute

As always, World IA Day is committed to being a welcoming environment. We strongly promote the inclusion of all people, regardless of gender, race, economic standing, or social background. By signing up to host World IA Day, we hope you agree to maintain and support our conference to this standard.
